# SEBS Design Roadmap Based on the initial architecture, here is the proposed plan to complete the high-fidelity design phase: ## Phase 1: Core Public Experience * **Event Details Page:** The conversion point. High-quality imagery, sticky booking card, and reviews. * **Registration Page:** Onboarding for both regular Users and Organizers. ## Phase 2: The User Journey (Attendee) * **User Dashboard:** "My Bookings" list with ticket downloads and cancellation options. * **Review Flow:** A dedicated modal or page for users to leave feedback on completed events. ## Phase 3: The Organizer Suite * **Organizer Dashboard:** High-level stats (Revenue, Tickets Sold) and event management table. * **Create/Edit Event:** A robust form with image uploads and scheduling. * **Attendee List:** A detailed view for organizers to manage their guest lists. ## Phase 4: System Administration * **Admin Dashboard:** System-wide overview and event approval queue. * **User Management:** Tools for managing roles and account statuses.
